<p>Vegan: End Animal Use</p>
<p>The vegan stance is that animals are not ours to use. Veganism means ending the use of animals in every form. It is not a diet. It is emancipation.</p>
<p>Harm and cruelty are just the symptoms of our relationship towards non-human animals. Reducing suffering is not the vegan goal. The root problem is that we see animals as slaves and resources to use in the first place.</p>
<p>Unlike animals, we are moral agents. Being vegan is giving animals basic respect, the least we should do.</p>
