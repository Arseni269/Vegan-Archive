<p>Animal use exists independently of political system. Veganism is not political in any way.</p>
<p>While it is true that some politicians and political parties might advocate for veganism, veganism itself has nothing to do with politics.</p>
<p>Veganism is about our relationship with non-human creatures. It is about rejecting human supremacy and respecting other sentient life forms.</p>
