<p>Veganism is mistakenly regarded as a preference because animals are still regarded as objects. And that is why veganism is so important.</p>
<p>For as long as we see living beings as things to be owned, altered, or consumed, moral thought cannot begin. The moment we recognize them as subjects, as lives that exist for themselves, the illusion of “preference” disappears, and what we find is only principle.</p>
