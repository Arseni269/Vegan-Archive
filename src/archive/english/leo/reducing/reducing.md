<p>Don't improve a system that should not exist.</p>
<p>Veganism is often seen as welfarist because most public messaging focuses on reducing visible suffering like better treatment, conditions, or slaughter methods rather than questioning animal use itself.
Because reforms are the most common and familiar entry point, veganism gets blurred with the idea of “improving” the system instead of challenging it.</p>
<p>Veganism is abolitionist by default.</p>
