<p>"Animals are here with us, not for us"
I have always loved this quote, it is so simple yet so clear on what veganism is. Veganism is not a set of practices, but an ethical principle where the practices follow, it is about NOT viewing animals as commodities. We have no right to use animals for our selfish gain.</p>

<p>"Veganism is the doctrine that man should live without exploiting animals." - The Vegan Story (1955) by Leslie Cross</p>
