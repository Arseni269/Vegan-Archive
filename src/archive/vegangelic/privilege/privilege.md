<p>Veganism is a privilege!”</p>

<p>It most certainly isn’t, but do you know what is a privilege?</p>

<p>Being able to view justice as optional.</p>

<p>Being able to sit so complacently in your position as an oppressor and feel like it’s acceptable for you to oppress because of pathetic, lousy excuses like “convenience” and “taste.”</p>

<p>Being born into the body of the species who isn’t a victim of the largest, most disgraceful act of injustice and oppression throughout all of history.</p>

<p>Being advantaged even within the human context yet exploiting the identities and plights of those who are less privileged than you as an excuse for you to continue oppressing non-human animals.</p>

<p>Being able to find yourself so concerned with vegans taking a stand for justice that you feel far more of a desire to spend time putting out disingenuous talking points like “Veganism is a privilege!” than you do to stop using your privilege to be an oppressor.</p>

<p>Please recognize your human privilege and stop using it as a reason to exploit and oppress the non-human.</p>
