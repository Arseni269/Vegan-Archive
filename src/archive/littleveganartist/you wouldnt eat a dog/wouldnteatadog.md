<p>Very often, we see posts saying "You wouldn't eat a dog, so why would you eat a pig?", giving the false impression that dogs are so well treated and respected. But, that is far from the truth. Dogs are still viewed as commodities for human use. Be it for entertainment, work, testing, sports, and also for food in many countries. As long as people still perceive animals as things for humans, the animals will never be emancipated. Go vegan, veganism is the principle that humanity should live without exploiting animals.
Veganism = End Animal Use.</p>
