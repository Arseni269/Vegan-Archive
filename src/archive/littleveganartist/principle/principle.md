<p>Veganism is itself a principle, from which certain practices naturally flow. It is the matter of the right relationship between man and the animals. Please remember this, veganism is a principle, and not a set a practices.</p>

<p>"Veganism is the doctrine that man should live without exploiting animals." - The Vegan Story (1955) by Leslie Cross</p>
